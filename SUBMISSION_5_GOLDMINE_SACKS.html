Executive Summary

SmartBite is an intelligent food inventory management platform designed to combat India's massive food wastage crisis. Through smart expiry tracking, AI-powered recipe recommendations, and multi-channel notifications, SmartBite prevents household food waste before it occurs.

🎯 Our Core Innovation

Unlike traditional reminder apps, SmartBite uses intelligent recipe matching to convert expiring food into delicious meals—transforming a "waste problem" into a "meal opportunity."

Problem Statement

India ranks among the top food-wasting nations globally. While 189.2 million Indians remain undernourished, households waste enough food annually to feed entire nations.

Root Causes Identified

❌ Lack of Visibility

Families forget what's stored in their fridge and pantry, leading to items being overlooked until expired.

❌ Poor Expiry Tracking

No systematic way to track expiry dates results in items expiring before consumption.

❌ Recipe Confusion

Not knowing what to cook with available ingredients leads to ingredients going unused.

❌ No Timely Reminders

Realizing food is expired only when it's already too late to use it.
